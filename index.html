Getting Started

Welcome

This is the Instruction Document for Rezdy Supplier Acquisition

The NoExpiry platform works along with the rezdy experience marketplace and as such adding additional suppliers from the Rezdy marketplace who are not already sharing their products with us is an important goal for us.

This Document will take you through all the steps required to approach suppliers within rezdy and invite them to join our amazing platform.

Content

This Documentation covers:

  • Logging into rezdy
  • Commonly Used Pages
  • Finding Suppliers
  • Determining Supplier Suitability
  • Requesting Supplier Negotiated Rates
  • Replying to Suppliers Who Have Provided Negotiated Rates

Introduction

Accessing Rezdy

In order to access Rezdy as a member of the NoExpiry team you must first have a set of login credentials, these should be set up by your supervisor.


First Time Login

When your supervisor first adds you to the noexpiry team you will receive an email like the one beneath, from this email click the link saying "Click here to choose password and login"

The following page should show up, when it does simply fill in the two fields with the new password you would like to use for your rezdy_account_email, Make sure your password is secure enough that it is not easy to guess yet memorable enough to ensure that you do not forget it yourself.


Returning User

If you are already a part of the NoExpiry Rezdy team navigate to https://app.rezdy.com/login You should find the page below, on this page enter your email address and password to login to Rezdy.

Rezdy Interface

After Successfully logging into the Rezdy website you should be presented with a rezdy dashboard like the one below.

Scrolling down the page a little we can see the notifications section on the right hand of the screen. This section will contain a list of actions which have been performed by noexpiry within rezdy and will have notifications when suppliers share or change their negotiated rates.

In the left hand navigation section we have access to the Dashboard, Marketplace, Orders, and, Customers. From these options we will be using the Dashboard and Marketplace sections of Rezdy for the moment.

If you click on the Marketplace item in the navigation menu You should find options for Tours and Activities, Find Suppliers, Find Agents, and, My Profile.

The Find Suppliers page allows us to search for Suppliers within the rezdy platform and will be the main focus of this document.

Finding Suppliers

Supplier Search

In order to find suppliers to invite to join the noexpiry platform we first need to go to the left side menu and select Marketplace > Find Suppliers.

You should then be faced with the find suppliers search page. On this page we can input several different search parameters and do a search for suppliers within the Rezdy marketplace.

The main parameter we are interested is the Rate parameter. This parameter allows us to filter the list of suppliers returned based on the current available rates which they have available for their products.

When we click on the rate drop down menu we can see the options:

  • All Suppliers - Does not filter by rates
  • Marketplace & Negotiated Rates - Shows only Suppliers with which we have Marketplace or Negotiated Rates
  • Marketplace Rate - Shows only Suppliers with which we have Marketplace rates
  • Negotiated Rates - Shows only Suppliers with which we have Negotiated rates
  • No Rate (Request) - Shows only Suppliers with which we have no rates
  • Rate Requested (Pending) - Shows only Suppliers which we have previously requested negotiated rates for

We are looking for suppliers which we do not already have any rates with so in this case we should select the No Rate option and since we are not wanting to filter the search any further we click on the search button.

Scrolling down a little on the page we should find a list of different suppliers as shown below.

Supplier Profile

Clicking on the name of any of the suppliers in the search results will take you to the suppliers profile page where we can view more information about the supplier and the products they offer.

We are currently in the overview section of the suppliers profile. The number of products a supplier has can be found on the right of this page and clicking on the word products in this section will take you to a search results page of all products from this supplier.

If we move to the rates section of the supplier profile by selecting the rates tab near the top of the profile we can view a list of the rates offered by the supplier. since we searched based on the supplier not having any rates accessible to us we should find a page listing the suppliers products with blank spaces in the negotiated rate column.

Some suppliers offer marketplace rates which NoExpiry currently honors. If there is any rates information under the marketplace rates column on this page then that supplier should be skipped as we already have a rate for them.

Evaluating Suppliers

Before we add suppliers products to our system we need to determine their suitability.

The suitability of suppliers can be determined by evaluating:

  • Product images - if a suppliers products are listed without having any images associated with them or a suppliers product images are of a considerably low quality they may not be suitable for the NoExpiry platform
  • Product descriptions - if a suppliers products are listed with descriptions which are too short or do not accurately describe the product their can be considered unsuitable

Below we have a comparison of two suppliers product lists showing the difference between a suitable and unsuitable supplier based on their product catalogue.

Good Product
These products are what good suppliers products should look like, all of these products have images and reasonably detailed product descriptions.
These products will look good on the platform and if the supplier accepts negotiated rates these products will be happily listed on the NoExpiry platform.



Bad Product
These suppliers products could be described as ill-suited for the NoExpiry platform, they contain products without images or products for which the images are merely the products name. product images should show the experience which is being provided for the product.

Product Descriptions are short and do not contain enough of a description about what the product is.


Requesting Suppliers Negotiated Rates

Requesting Negotiated Rates

Once we have examined the suppliers products we can send them a request for negotiated rates.

The commission which NoExpiry works with is a flat 10% rate and we request this or higher from all suppliers on all products they supply. This rate will be stated in our agent profile and suppliers should be directed to read the agent profile when viewing our request for negotiated rates.

To Request the negotiated rates for a supplier we must go to the suppliers profile. This can be accessed be either going back in the browser or clicking on the suppliers name in the bottom left hand corner of one of their products.

On the supplier profile we need to select the rates tab just under the suppliers name


We can then select the Negotiated rates dropdown and from there select the button which says Send Request.

The next page we will come to should look like the one below. In this page we want to input a message to the supplier giving them a reason to share negotiated rate with us. There will be a templated message set up for you to send in this section which will be provided to you by your supervisor. After we input our message from the template we simply click on the 'Send the Request' button and our request for negotiated rates will be sent to the supplier.

The negotiated rate should now be sent. The next step is to take note of the suppliers status using the form available at https://chokilatrain.github.io/rezdy-supplier-form/. Here you should enter the suppliers alias from rezdy, You can find this on the suppliers profile page in the url bar as shown in the image below.

After entering this into the space provided click the search button next to the input and the form will automatically fill in the suppliers name and display their logo.
Confirm that this is the supplier you are trying to input and then scroll down to the next section of the form.

In this section of the form you will need to tick the applicable answers to the questions about the supplier. First of all tick whether or not the suppliers products all had resonable images and descriptions. Then tick any applicable categories that suppliers products might fall under from the next three questions. The last section is where you would place any notes about the supplier that you think might be important, possible notes could include: "there was only one product out of 10 products the supplier has that is a transport product", or, "Supplier has 230 products", really it is up to your discretion what you write as a note and not all suppliers need to have a note. If a supplier has good images and has some products which are transport or transfers then they would look like the following.

Finally, when you think you have answered the form as accurately as possible click on the submit button at the bottom of the page. This will reload the page and give you a message at the top of the page about the success of the submission. If you can not remember if you have submitted a status form about a supplier it is recommended to simply submit another one. The page will notify you if a supplier has already been added and no negative effect will occur.

Responding to Suppliers

When a supplier has received our request for negotiated rates they may choose to send us some negotiated rates. When a supplier does share their negotiated rates you will receive an email from rezdy notifying you of this. In this email you can click where it says 'profile' to be directed to the suppliers profile page where you can find their contact email address to respond to.

Within this email you should also be able to see the commission percentages which the supplier has offered NoExpiry.

There are 3 different situations which can occur after sending a supplier a request for negotiated rates:

The supplier sends us negotiated rates with 15% or more commission percentage

If the supplier decides to send us Negotiated Rate which have a commission rate of 15% or more for each we will be required to send the supplier an email thanking them for joining the NoExpiry platform and welcoming them.

The supplier sends us negotiated rates with under 15% commission percentage

If the supplier decides to send us Negotiated Rate which have a commission rate of less than 15% we need to send them an email notifying them that their products will not be able to be listed on the NoExpiry platform until they can provide a negotiated rate for them of 15% or greater.

The supplier emails you a query about our platform

A supplier may require more information about NoExpiry before sending us Negotiated Rates and may send you an email with an enquiry. In this case we are required to send an email in response notifying them that their enquiry has been received and will be looked into. enquiries which are simple may be answerable by you but for anything which you feel you do not know the answer to please send a copy of the enqury to the devteam@noexpiry.com email address and the development team will take a look into handling the enquiry for you.

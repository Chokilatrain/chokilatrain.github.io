Getting Started

Welcome

This is the Instruction Document for Rezdy Supplier Acquisition

The NoExpiry platform works along with the rezdy experience marketplace and as such adding additional suppliers from the Rezdy marketplace who are not already sharing their products with us is an important goal for us.

This Document will take you through all the steps required to approach suppliers within rezdy and invite them to join our amazing platform.

Introduction

Accessing Rezdy

In order to access Rezdy as a member of the NoExpiry team you must first have a set of login credentials, these should be set up by your supervisor.


First Time Login

When your supervisor first adds you to the noexpiry team you will receive an email like the one beneath, from this email click the link saying "Click here to choose password and login"

The following page should show up, when it does simply fill in the two fields with the new password you would like to use for your rezdy_account_email, Make sure your password is secure enough that it is not easy to guess yet memorable enough to ensure that you do not forget it yourself.


Returning User

If you are already a part of the NoExpiry Rezdy team navigate to https://app.rezdy.com/login You should find the page below, on this page enter your email address and password to login to Rezdy.

Rezdy Interface

After Successfully logging into the Rezdy website you should be presented with a rezdy dashboard like the one below.

Scrolling down the page a little we can see the notifications section on the right hand of the screen. This section will contain a list of actions which have been performed by noexpiry within rezdy and will have notifications when suppliers share or change their negotiated rates.

In the left hand navigation section we have access to the Dashboard, Marketplace, Orders, and, Customers. From these options we will be using the Dashboard and Marketplace sections of Rezdy for the moment.

If you click on the Marketplace item in the navigation menu You should find options for Tours and Activities, Find Suppliers, Find Agents, and, My Profile.

The Find Suppliers page allows us to search for Suppliers within the rezdy platform and will be the main focus of this document.

Finding Suppliers

Supplier Search

In order to find suppliers to invite to join the noexpiry platform we first need to go to the left side menu and select Marketplace > Find Suppliers.

You should then be faced with the find suppliers search page. On this page we can input several different search parameters and do a search for suppliers within the Rezdy marketplace.

The main parameter we are interested is the Rate parameter. This parameter allows us to filter the list of suppliers returned based on the current available rates which they have available for their products.

When we click on the rate drop down menu we can see the options:

  • All Suppliers - Does not filter by rates
  • Marketplace & Negotiated Rates - Shows only Suppliers with which we have Marketplace or Negotiated Rates
  • Marketplace Rate - Shows only Suppliers with which we have Marketplace rates
  • Negotiated Rates - Shows only Suppliers with which we have Negotiated rates
  • No Rate (Request) - Shows only Suppliers with which we have no rates
  • Rate Requested (Pending) - Shows only Suppliers which we have previously requested negotiated rates for

We are looking for suppliers which we do not already have any rates with so in this case we should select the No Rate option and since we are not wanting to filter the search any further we click on the search button.

Scrolling down a little on the page we should find a list of different suppliers as shown below.

Supplier Profile

Clicking on the name of any of the suppliers in the search results will take you to the suppliers profile page where we can view more information about the supplier and the products they offer.

We are currently in the overview section of the suppliers profile. The number of products a supplier has can be found on the right of this page and clicking on the word products in this section will take you to a search results page of all products from this supplier.

If we move to the rates section of the supplier profile by selecting the rates tab near the top of the profile we can view a list of the rates offered by the supplier. since we searched based on the supplier not having any rates accessible to us we should find a page listing the suppliers products with blank spaces in the marketplace rate / negotiated rate column

From this page we can request this suppliers negotiated rates, but first we need to make sure that this supplier is suitable to join the NoExpiry platform. This will be the focus of the next section of the document.

Evaluating Suppliers

Before we invite a supplier to share their negotiated rates with NoExpiry we need to take a look at the suppliers product list and determine their Suitability.

The suitability of suppliers can be determined by evaluating:

  • Product images - if a suppliers products are listed without having any images associated with them or a suppliers product images are of a considerably low quality they may not be suitable for the NoExpiry platform
  • Profanity - if a supplier provides products which contain profanity in name or description they may not be suitable for the NoExpiry platform
  • Nudity - if a suppliers product images contain graphic nudity they may not be suitable for the NoExpiry platform
